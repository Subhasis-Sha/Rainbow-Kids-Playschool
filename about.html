Our Milestone History

How We Grew & Bloomed

2020

The First Seeds Seeded

GVIT-Playschool opened its gates with 12 students in a cozy garden home cottage, emphasizing play-based routines.

2022

A Growing Family

Built our current large building block adding slide playgrounds, classroom modules, and child-safe library shelves.

2024

Awarded Play Space

Won the National Early Education Design award for child safety integration and creative toy setups.

Vibrant Classrooms

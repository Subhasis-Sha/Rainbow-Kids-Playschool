Our Programs

Explore our age-based curriculum structures. We ensure smooth milestone transits from early play to structured classroom readiness.

Nursery Playpen Tier

For toddlers taking their first steps away from home. We emphasize sensory explorations, potty training transitions, verbal expressions, and basic motor co-ordination.

Focus Areas:

  • 🍭 Sensory Integration: Sandbox, clay models, water splash play.
  • 🎨 Finger Painting: Hand prints, coloring books, large block drawing.
  • 🎵 Nursery Rhymes: Music listening, clapping games, rhythmic dance.

LKG Explorer Tier

For children learning to socialize, count, and structure their thoughts. We introduce writing lines, basic spelling, arithmetic (1-20), and cognitive block matching.

Focus Areas:

  • 🅰️ Letters & Sounds: Phonics sounds, writing shapes, identifying letters.
  • 🔢 Early Math: Number drawings, puzzle matchings, size orders.
  • 🤝 Socialization: Story rotations, toy sharing, peer greetings.

UKG Graduate Tier

Prepares children for standard elementary schools. We master reading basic sentences, solving addition/subtraction, writing words, and handling independent projects.

Focus Areas:

  • 📖 Active Reading: Sight words, reading stories, spelling.
  • ➕ Elementary Math: Adding objects, subtractions, counting (1-100).
  • 💡 General Science: Seasons, animals, plants, health hygiene.
